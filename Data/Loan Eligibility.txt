Loan Eligibility:
- Applicant must be at least 21 years old
- Minimum monthly income: $2,500
- No default history in the past 12 months

Repayment Terms:
- Payments are due monthly
- Late payments are charged a 3% penalty
- Default occurs if payment is missed for more than 60 days

Reapplication:
- Rejected applicants must wait 30 days before reapplying
